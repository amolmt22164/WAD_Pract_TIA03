Delicious Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Steps to Prepare

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, and stir until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour batter onto the skillet and cook until bubbles form.
  6. Flip and cook until golden brown on both sides.

Recipe Description

This pancake recipe is simple, quick, and perfect for a tasty breakfast. Serve with syrup, fruits, or whipped cream for extra flavor.
